This project will collect three categories of data: financial market data, macroeconomic data, and sentiment data.
1. Financial Market Data: 
JPM Stock Price: Daily open, high, low, close, adjusted close, and trading volume of JPMorgan Chase (JPM).
VIX Index: Daily market volatility index published by CBOE, commonly used as a measure of market uncertainty.
2. Macroeconomic Data：
Treasury Rate: Daily U.S. Treasury interest rates, which can be used as a proxy for the risk-free rate in option pricing models.
3. Sentiment Data：
Market Sentiment Indicators: Quantitative measures representing positive, neutral, or negative market sentiment.

Timeline: This investigation is only based on daily data from 2018-2024.
